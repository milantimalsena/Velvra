About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Milan Timalsena, Velvra was created with a passion for stylish, high-quality shoes. We believe that the right pair of shoes can transform confidence and comfort. Our collection brings the perfect blend of innovation, fashion, and durability. At Velvra, we are committed to delivering the best in footwear with unique designs, premium materials, and cutting-edge technology. Thank you for being part of our journey.

Learn More About Our Founder

Milan Timalsena, the visionary behind Velvra, has always been passionate about footwear design. With a background in fashion and a keen eye for detail, Milan aims to create shoes that not only look good but also feel great. Under his leadership, Velvra is committed to pushing the boundaries of shoe design and sustainability.
